Behind the Screen

I started as a player who couldn't stop wondering how it all worked under the hood. That curiosity became a choice, then a passion, and finally a craft.

I study Game Development at HEAJ in Belgium, focusing on gameplay programming — turning ideas into things people can actually feel. Mechanics that carry emotion. Systems that tell stories.

I released Lampyris Moon on Steam in May 2026, a solo-developed narrative psychological horror game. Going through the full cycle — from concept to live product — taught me more than any course could.
